Why We Give

Giving is an act of worship and a way to support the ministry and mission of GraceCommunity Church. Your generous contributions enable us to:

  • Support our community outreach programs
  • Maintain our facilities and resources
  • Provide pastoral care and counseling
  • Fund missions and global ministry
  • Equip believers for service

Ways to Give

Online Giving

Secure online giving is available through our payment gateway. You can set up one-time or recurring donations.

In Person

You can give during our Sunday services or visit our office during business hours. We accept cash, checks, and card payments.

By Mail

Send your contribution to our church address. Please make checks payable to GraceCommunity Church.
