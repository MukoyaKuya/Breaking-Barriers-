Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting our website. At bb-international.org we are committed to protecting your privacy and confidentiality of your personal information. This privacy policy statement explains how we collect, use and safeguard your information when you use our website.

Collection of personal information

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, mailing address and phone number when you sign up for our newsletters, events register or make donations.

We may also collect nonpersonal information such as IP address, browser type and device type when you visit our website

Use of Personal

The personal details you are giving will not be shared anywhere but only used internally with BBI for purposes of reaching out and praying with you.

We may use your personal information to provide you with updates on our ministries, events and other resources that may be of interest to you. We may also use your personal information to process donations, respond to your queries and improve our website user experience.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We will not disclose your personal information to any third parties except as required by law. We may share your personal information with our trusted service providers who assist us in providing services to you, such as processing donations or sending newsletters.

Security of Personal Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. We use SSL encryption technology to protect your information during transmission and implement security measures to protect your information stored on our servers.

Use of Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your user experience on our website. Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. We use cookies to remember your preferences, provide personalized content, and track website usage

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy statement from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this statement periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy statement, please contact us at info@bb-international.org

We will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.

Thank you for trusting us with your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your personal information secure.
