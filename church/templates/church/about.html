About Us

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in our community, where every person experiences the love of Christ and discovers their God-given purpose. We envision a church that bridges generations, cultures, and backgrounds in unified worship and service.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ by sharing the Gospel, nurturing spiritual growth through authentic community, and serving our neighbors with compassion and grace. We are committed to equipping believers to impact their families, workplaces, and communities for God's kingdom.

Our History

GraceCommunity Church was founded with a vision to create a welcoming space where individuals and families can grow in their faith journey. Since our establishment, we have been committed to serving our community and sharing the message of God's love.

Over the years, we have seen lives transformed, families strengthened, and our community enriched through various outreach programs, worship services, and fellowship opportunities. We believe in the power of authentic relationships and genuine care for one another.

Today, we continue to build on this foundation, reaching out to new generations while honoring the traditions that have shaped us. We invite you to be part of our story and experience the joy of belonging to a community that truly cares.

Leadership Team

Our leadership team is dedicated to serving the congregation and guiding the church's mission. They bring years of experience, wisdom, and a heart for ministry.

We are blessed with pastors, elders, and ministry leaders who are committed to shepherding the flock and equipping the saints for the work of ministry. Their passion for God's word and love for people is evident in everything they do.
